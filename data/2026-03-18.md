(3) Lightweight models (e.g., Gemini~2.0, GPT~3.5) offer favorable trade-offs in accuracy, cost, and latency; SotA models yield higher accuracy at significantly greater cost and latency. Our study provides tools, methodologies, and insights for leveraging LLMs to accurately and efficiently extract incident-report information. The FAIR data and code are publicly available at https://github.com/atlarge-research/llm-cloud-incident-extraction.
